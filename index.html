Theoric is an applied AI lab focused on the intersection of formal methods and agentic AI.

We believe that by combining the rigid structures of formal methods with the ability of AI systems to show intelligent behavior in situations involving uncertainty and ambiguity, we can achieve a lot more than either can achieve by itself.

Read the blog to know more about our philosophy, and reach out to team@theoric.com for questions or discussion on our product offerings or research.
